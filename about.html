About the Table Cloth Project

Welcome to the Embroidery Stories website! This space is dedicated to preserving and sharing the stories stitched into our embroidered tablecloth. Each contributor weaves their personal connection to the land into every thread, turning fabric into a tapestry of meaning, memory, and passion.

Our Journey

What began as a handful of small embroidered images has blossomed into a vibrant community, bound together by our shared experiences and artistic expressions. From pre-pandemic times to today’s evolving world, our tablecloth has grown—each stitch telling a unique story of place, identity, and creativity.

Meet the Contibutors

Our storytellers come from diverse backgrounds, each bringing a deep appreciation for art, storytelling, and placemaking. Together, we create something greater than ourselves—one stitch at a time.
